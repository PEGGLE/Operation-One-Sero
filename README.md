# Operation One ESP

A Serotonin script for Operation One that draws ESP for players, cameras, drones, and bomb (+ defuser).

---

## General

- **Box Style** - choose between Normal (full rectangle), Corner (corner brackets only), or Bracket (side rails with partial top and bottom lines)
- **Outline** - adds a black outline around all boxes for better visibility

---

## Player ESP

- **Players** - toggles the entire player ESP on or off
- **Player Boxes** - draws a box around enemy players using whichever box style is selected
- **Box Color** - sets the color of the player box (default red)
- **Player Names** - displays the player's username above their box
- **Text Color** - sets the color of name and distance text
- **Player Distance** - shows how far away the player is in meters
- **Player Health Bar** - draws a vertical green health bar on the left side of the box that scales with current HP

Players on your own team are skipped automatically.

---

## Camera ESP

- **Cameras** - toggles camera ESP on or off
- **Camera Boxes** - draws a box around the camera model using its parts, otherwise shows text at the camera position
- **Box Color** - sets the color of camera boxes (default purple)
- **Camera Names** - displays the camera model name
- **Text Color** - sets the color of name and distance text
- **Camera Distance** - shows distance to the camera in meters

---

## Drone ESP

- **Drones** - toggles drone ESP on or off
- **Drone Boxes** - draws a box around the drone model, otherwise shows text at the drone's root position
- **Box Color** - sets the color of drone boxes (default blue)
- **Drone Names** - displays the drone name
- **Text Color** - sets the color of name and distance text
- **Drone Distance** - shows distance to the drone in meters

---

## Bomb ESP

- **Bomb** - toggles bomb and defuser ESP on or off
- **Bomb Boxes** - draws a box around the bomb and defuser model, otherwise shows text at its root position
- **Box Color** - sets the color of bomb boxes (default pink)
- **Bomb Names** - displays whether the object is a Bomb or Defuser
- **Text Color** - sets the color of name and distance text
- **Bomb Distance** - shows distance to the bomb or defuser in meters
